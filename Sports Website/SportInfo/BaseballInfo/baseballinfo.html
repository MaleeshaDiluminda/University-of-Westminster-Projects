BASEBALL

This game is played by, Two teams of and each team has 9 players and there are also 4 umpires.
TUmpires watch everything carefully to decide what happened, make calls about a play, and make sure everyone follows the rules.
On a baseball field, there are four bases. The bases form a diamond that goes around the field to the right from the starting base.
The starting base is called home plate. Home plate is a pentagon, which is a shape that has five sides.
IFirst base is on the right side of the field, second base is at the top of the infield, third base is on the left side of the field, and home plate is at the back of the field, where the catcher plays.
The game is played in innings. Professional baseball games have 9 innings.
In an inning, each team has one turn to bat and try to score runs, adding one point.

When the team on defense gets three players out, it is their turn to try and score runs.



FACTS!

Baseball - Baseball, game played with a bat, a ball, and gloves between two teams of nine players each on a field with four white bases laid out in a diamond (i.e., a square oriented so that its diagonal line is vertical). Teams alternate positions as batters (offense) and fielders (defense), exchanging places when three members of the batting team are “put out.” As batters, players try to hit the ball out of the reach of the fielding team and make a complete circuit around the bases for a “run.” The team that scores the most runs in nine innings (times at bat) wins the game.



MLB

The Major League Baseball (MLB) is an American men's professional baseball league. It is composed of 30 teams (29 in the United States and 1 in Canada)

and is one of the four major professional sports leagues in the United States and Canada. It is widely considered to be the premier men's professional baseball league in the world.
The MLB is an active member of USA Baseball (USAB),which is recognized by FIBA (also known as the International Basetball Federation)

as the national governing body for baseball in the United States.
The MLB is the second wealthiest professional sport league after the NFL and the NBL by revenue.
