Cricket

Basically cricket is a sport which is played by 2 teams consisting 11 players each. And the game is won by the team who gets the highest score,
which is done by running between two sets of three small, wooden posts called wickets.

There're two wickets at the each end of the pitch. Pitch is the flat and short cropped grass surface at the field(rectangular).

And a 30 yard circle inside the ground or the stadium is the area of play. This game
strated in the 16th century, in England. Later, the game spread to countries of the British Empire in the 19th and 20th centuries.

ICC

The International Cricket Council is the world governing body of cricket.
It took up it's current name in 1989. And was founded as the imperial Cricket conference in 1909.

by representatives from Australia, England and South Africa.

It was also renamed as the International Cricket Conference in 1965, before having the current name.


There're 104 countries that are associated to the ICC.But with the total of 12 Full Members.
